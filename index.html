Chennai, 23rd July 2025: India Yamaha Motor Pvt Ltd (IYM) has reached a significant milestone with the inauguration of its 500th Blue Square showroom in India. This marks a strong step forward in Yamaha’s customer-centric growth journey, especially in creating exclusive experiences tailored for premium customers. The expansion reinforces Yamaha’s nationwide reach in sales, service, and support, while accelerating its strategy in the premium two-wheeler segment.

Since the introduction of the ‘The Call of the Blue’ brand campaign in 2018, Yamaha has consistently focused on offering products that appeal to the aspirations of India’s new-age riders and premium customers. The Blue Square showrooms—launched in 2019—have played a central role in this vision, serving as exclusive, experiential spaces that reflect Yamaha’s racing DNA and performance-oriented legacy. “Blue Square” blends Yamaha's global motorsports heritage with a one-stop buying experience— ‘Blue’ symbolizing its racing lineage and ‘Square’ creating a vibrant hub for customers to engage with Yamaha’s aesthetics and exciting offerings.
